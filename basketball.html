Recruiting Boost has had great success in placing basketball players from all over the world to all levels of college basketball from High Major Division 1 to Junior College.

Recruiting Boost’s Founder, Cassius Bosket, is a former Division 1 coach with experience at Presbyterian College (SC), Miami University (OH), and Clemson University.

Recruiting Boost prides itself on NOT using cold email blasts or databases. We don’t use cold emails because most coaches don’t open cold emails and your individual talent level, grades, and character make you a perfect fit for a select number of colleges, rather than all of the colleges. If you’re a Power 5 player, there is no need to email D3’s. If you’re a D2 player, there is no need to email Power 5 schools. We rely on DIRECT CONTACT to college coaches that match your specific talent through calls, texts, and social media.

FAQ

  • What college coaches do you have connections with?

    We currently have contacts in the following leagues: ACC, SEC, Big 12, Big 10, Big South, SoCon, Horizon, MAC, CAA, Conference USA, Ivy League, OVC, A10, Sun Belt, Peach Belt, Gulf South, SAC & many more D2, D3, NAIA, and JUCO leagues. We continue to build trustworthy relationships with new college coaches daily.

  • How fast can I get a quote?

    Call, text or email us directly for a same-day quote.

  • Can you guarantee an offer?

    It is currently against NCAA rules for a recruiting service to offer a refund should a client not receive an offer and payment cannot be contingent upon an offer. Therefore, we can't tell you to pay us only when a school offers nor can we refund your money if you do not receive an offer. However, if we take you on as a client we have full confidence in being able to help you have a successful recruitment.
