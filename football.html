Football

We are very excited to add football to the sports we offer services in. Recruiting Boost looks for talent, character, and desire in the clients we represent and help navigate through the recruiting process.

The football recruiting process is very different than most sports. Social media potential, camps/combines, and all-star games are all a part of the journey, but the most IMPORTANT piece is direct contact to coaches.

Recruiting Boost prides itself on NOT using cold email blasts or databases. We don’t use cold emails because most coaches don’t open cold emails and your individual talent level, grades, and character makes you a perfect fit for a select number of colleges, rather than all of the colleges. If you’re a Power 5 player, there is no need to email D3’s. If you’re a D2 player, there is no need to email Power 5 schools. We rely on DIRECT CONTACT to college coaches that match your specific talent through calls, texts, and social media.

NCAA Division 1 FBS schools are the only ones not allowed to give partial scholarships and exclusively offer 85 full-ride scholarships. D1 FCS can distribute 63 scholarships however they wish and D2 coaches can offer a max of 36. NAIA’s are limited to 24 total scholarships, while junior colleges can offer up to 85.

  • How fast can I get a quote?

    Call, text or email us directly for a same-day quote.

  • Can you guarantee an offer?

    It is currently against NCAA rules for a recruiting service to offer a refund should a client not receive an offer and payment cannot be contingent upon an offer. Therefore, we can't tell you to pay us only when a school offers nor can we refund your money if you do not receive an offer. However, if we take you on as a client we have full confidence in being able to help you have a successful recruitment.
